A synopsis of my musical endeavors.

Bands I’ve played drums in…

Babe

I was a performing and recording member of Babe from 2015 to 2017.

Listen to Babe on their website, iTunes and Spotify.

Moving Castles

I was the original drummer for Moving Castles before the band moved to Austin, TX around 2013.

My recording contributions can be heard on the Early Demos EP, and on the songs Warhol and Hush My Bones.

Warhol can be heard on the following TV Series:

  • New Girl
    • Season 3, Episode 2
      • The song is playing in the background during the bar scene with Jess and Nick.
  • Switched at Birth
    • Season 2, Episode 20
      • Listen around 30:00
  • Shameless
    • Season3, Episode 4
  • The Fosters
    • Season 3, Episode 6

Ivoryline

I played drums in Dead End Driveway before the band became Ivoryline. See the members section of Ivoryline’s Wikipedia page.

Info and media can be found on Wikipedia, Spotify and Apple Music.

Solo

Now that I’m bandless, I play the occasional solo acoustic set at coffee shops and open mics around town. I’ll post recordings of these as they come available.
